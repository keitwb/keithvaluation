Tom J. Keith & Associates, Inc. is a 40 year old 10-member commercial and business valuation firm serving individuals and many Fortune 500 firms and governments in the Southeast US. The members hold State and MAI certifications in commercial real estate and also ASA and CBA certifications in Business Valuations. Work has been performed on over 15,000 valuations amounting to several billion dollars for individual investors, developers, buyers, sellers, lenders, lessors and lessee, business owners and operators, CPA's, attorneys, lenders, for loan, gift, damage estimates, loss profits, sale, litigation, equitable distribution, contamination from industrial products, damages caused by proximity from quarries, mining operations, cell towers, land fills, and other adverse uses.
Specific assignments have included valuations of office buildings, restaurants, medical clinics, mobile home parks, petroleum facilities, manufacturing and warehouse facilities, large land tracts, wetlands, conservation easements, mining operations and other special purpose properties.

The firm utilizes the latest technology in software, GIS data, and mapping equipment.
