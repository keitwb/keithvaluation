Employment

Tom J. Keith & Associates is always looking for qualified individuals. Applicants should be a college graduate or have Appraisal Experience. Preferred degrees include Business Administration, Economics, Finance, Accounting, Engineering, Geography, Math, Statistics, Construction, Surveying, Appraising, Meteorology, Music, Forestry, Computer Science, and Other Technical Degrees.

Upon Being Accepted for Employment, Applicant must have the following:
  • Real Estate Appraisal Trainee License
  • Real Estate Broker's License
  • The applicant should have completed the following courses required for the Certified General Appraiser License:
